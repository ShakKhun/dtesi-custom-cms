9th International Conference Digital Technologies in Education, Science and Industry

November 19-20, 2025, Almaty

Venue

Almaty is Kazakhstan's financial, scientific, cultural, economic, historical, and industrial centre. The city of Almaty has always been considered a garden city, surrounded by magnificent Tien Shan firs. For many centuries in a row, the snowy peaks of the mighty Zaili Alatau, soaring up to the sky, create the impression of a majestic guard. The fragrance of gardens, the emerald tops of the famous Alatau firs and poplars, swift mountain currents, an abundance of bustling markets, the reviving freshness of city fountains, and the brilliance of palaces and squares make an unforgettable impression on the guests of the southern capital - Almaty.

JSC "International University of Information Technologies" (IITU) - the leading institution of higher education in Central Asia in the field of training qualified and internationally recognized IT specialists. The university was established on April 2, 2009, on behalf of the First President of Kazakhstan – N.A. Nazarbayev. The university forms a new generation of specialists in the field of ICT with knowledge of industry technologies, advanced management, economics, and communication skills with in-depth knowledge of the English language.
